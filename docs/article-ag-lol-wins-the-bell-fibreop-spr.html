AG LoL wins the Bell FibreOP Spring Cup 2013

Article


In partnership with Bell and FragForCharity, the FibreOP Spring Cup 2013 featured two e-sport tournament today : Starcraft 2 and League of Legends. Our team fought the University of Waterloo in the semi-finale, which they won 2-0.

The finale was a lot more tricky against the Toronto Team Two, who won the first match after an unexpected comeback. Armata replied with an epic snowballing setup during the second match. Gratz to Veelox, Sarasun, Doomtrobo, Consommak et Zhanos for showing up strong during the last match and seal the deal for a 1000$ prize.
