Talents

Pet Talents
Ferocity

Mage Méga giga nerf triple
Deep Freeze now has a 4 sec stun, down from 5 sec.
Frost Bomb now takes 6 sec to explode, up from 5 sec.

Talents

Fire
Pyroblast now has a 3 sec cooldown.


Monk Nerf healing
Healing Sphere now lasts 10 sec, down from 2 min.

Mistweaver
Mastery: Gift of the Serpent now has a 60 sec duration for the summoned Healing Sphere.


Priest BUFF Disc et autres... surtout Mass Dispell va devenir OP
Mass Dispel now removes all beneficial spells from each enemy target, up from 1.
Penance now heals for 6,606 + 63.5% of SP, up from 1,000 + 103.7% of SP.

Discipline
Penance now heals for 6,606 + 63.5% of SP, up from 1,000 + 103.7% of SP.

Rogue Buffing, hard sur le blind surtout
Blind now has a 1 min cooldown, down from 3 min.
Sprint now has a 45 sec cooldown, down from 60 sec.
Wound Poison damage increased by 33%.

Talents
Burst of Speed now costs 50 energy, down from 60.
Shuriken Toss damage is doubled if the enemy is farther than 10 yards away.


Warlock Les locks vont pouvoir caster en courant maintenant sans réduction de casting time. MEGA BUFF.

Talents
Kil'jaeden's Cunning no longer increases the cast time of the spell.

Affliction
Soul Swap (Affliction) - Soulburn: Applies Corruption, Unstable Affliction and Agony without removing them from a target.

Destruction
Cataclysm was reworked, see tooltip.
Conflagrate now generates Burning Embers. Critical strikes double this effect. BUFF
Flames of Xoroth no longer has a cooldown.


Warrior NERF NERF NERF (fini le silence OP... on pourra vous juke maintenant !!!)

Glyph of Gag Order no longer works against players.
