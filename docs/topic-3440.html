Just to add in some mechanic info on darktide.

the Flash freeze has 2 versions, one when undertow is surged other is when normal, the surged version has a stun and start to dmg right the way, the unsurged version u have 3 second or so to move around.

Both undertow and rot jaw can be "cleansed", basicly u need to drag them through a geyser circle(bluE) that is around on the platform.

when undertow is cleansed he loses surge instantly, and when rot jaw is cleansed he gets a debuff called washed clean and no longer does aoe.

obv these mechanics i mentioned can be bruteforced, just thought as a guide it should atleast list its complete mechanics.
