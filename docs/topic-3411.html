Salut,

J'ai fait une petite recherche pour savoir quel classe possède un healing reduction pour pouvoir en stack sur le boss pour ainsi ignorer totalement les adds.


Quote: Champion
Lingering Wounds (Requires 15 points, 2 ranks) - Critical hits reduce healing on target by 25/50% for 15 seconds.

Quote: Nightblade
Fell Blade (32 point root ability) - The Rogue enchants their weapons with Death magic. Each weapon attack has a 20% chance to deal 29 Death damage and adds a curse on the enemy that lasts for 15 seconds, reducing healing taken by 50%. Lasts 1 hour. Only 2 weapon enchantments can be applied at a time.

Quote: Dominator
Void Shroud (18 point root ability) - 10 sec cooldown - Curses the enemy, reducing all healing they receive by 50% for 10 seconds.

Quote: Chloromancer
Blight: Requires 38 points spent in the Chloromancer soul. - 30.00 Seconds Cooldown - Strips all heal over time effects from the enemy and reduces healing they receive by 50% for 8 seconds.

Quote: Dominator
Chastise: Requires 30 points spent in the Dominator soul. - 30.00 Seconds Cooldown - Curses the enemy for 20 seconds, inflicting a penalty on them each time they perform an action, placing a stacking debuff on them reducing their damage and healing for 6 seconds by 10% per stack. Max 10 stacks. This ability can only be used on players and player pets.

Draining Presence: Requires 51 points spent in the Dominator soul. - no cooldown - Projects the Mage's presence on the target, draining the energy from enemies within 10 meters. Each time an affected enemy performs an action they receive a stacking debuff, reducing their damage and healing by 10% for 6 seconds per stack. Max 10 stacks. This ability consumes Charge while active. Charge cannot be gained while active. This ability only effects players and player pets.

Quote: Necromancer
Essence Link: Requires 10 points spent in the Necromancer soul. - No Cooldown - Reduces any healing the enemy receives by 30% for 60 seconds. Causes undead pets to heal the pet owner for 6 health each time the pet deals damage.

Quote: Archmage
Withered Veins: Requires 15 points spent in the Archmage soul. - Your single target non-instant damage spells add Withered Veins to players, reducing healing received by 5%. Stacks up to 5 times. Last for 10 seconds.

Quote: Infiltrator
Anathema: Requires 15 points spent in the Infiltrator soul. - 30.00 Seconds Cooldown - Reduces the healing done by the enemy player by 50%. Each successful heal reduces the effect by 10%. Lasts up to 30 seconds.

Quote: Druid
Spiteful Spirit: Requires 44 points spent in to the Druid soul. - 8.00 Seconds Cooldown - A malevolent strike that calls on a spiteful spirit to hinder aid given to the enemy, dealing weapon plus 76 to 79 Physical damage, in addition to causing healing abilities used on them to heal 50% less health for 15 seconds.

Quote: Templar
Retribution: Requires 20 points spent in the Templar soul. - 2 Minutes Cooldown - Transfers 50% of all healing used on the enemy to the Cleric. Lasts 15 seconds.
