Message #9 le 2012-7-12 @ 18:47:40 par Alpro:
http://wiki.guildwars2.com/wiki/Talk:Skill_point

"So you get a total of 76 points for leveling, possibly more if you keep earning them after level 80 (like GW1). The skill challenge page says that there were 182 skill challenges in the BWE. Which is more than enough to buy every profession and racial skill. So the real question is, what do we do with our excess skill points ?"

Faque 5 points pour 5 skills tier 1
+15 points pour 5 skills tier 2
+15 points pour 3 skills tier 3
+30 points pour un élite
+5 pour le self-heal le plus cher

Dans le "pire" des cas, avec trois skills utilitaires tier 3, le heal le plus cher et un élite, ça donne 70 points, soit, level 74 (sans challenges).
