A quick update for the end of this week:

Primalist: we are currently testing the primalist on our local servers, it's looking good and we believe it's going to be a great addition to the game.

We're looking into a salvaging system. We've noticed suggestions to this effect by the community for a long time now, and we're working on it.

Another community discussion which coincided with internal developer discussions and designs for the game: A system where your character progression will depend on prowess points. This is in the works.

Also, we have a patch next week which will be discussing in the following days.

In anticipation of comments to this effect: It might seem that the things we mentioned in this post will take a long time to implement and test in order to make it into the game before launch, but this is not the case.

Thanks for reading and have a nice weekend.
