River of Soul - Warmater Galenir

Message #1 le 2011-5-1 @ 4:53:59 par Hertuu:
Unstable video.
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qpuFz-FAqTE&feature=player_embedded

Health: 3,114,072


Enrage: 5 minutes, 30 seconds



Abilities:

Rend Flesh: Bleed applied to the tank. Deals 700-1k damage a tick.


Infecting Strike: Applied a stacking debuff to the tank. At 10 stacks, the tank explodes and wipes the raid. Lasts 60 seconds. Deals 2-3k damage a swing.



Howl of Empowerment: Deals minor AoE damage to the raid.



Howl of Death: Used every 10 seconds after he transforms into a werewolf. Deals ~2200 damage to the raid.



Disturbing Howl: Single target fear used on 1 random raid member every minute or so. Deals 1200 damage.



Discordant Infection: Spreads from Discordant Contagion. Deals ~1k damage per tick per stack. Can be dispelled.



Discordant Contagion: Used on the raid member that is the furthest from the boss. When they have this debuff they spread Discordant Infection to nearby raid members. Deals an initial 600 damage.



Deadly Barrage: Used every 10 seconds. Hits 12 raid members for ~2800 damage.



Charge: Charges the furthest member when he applies Discordant Contagion. Deals ~900 damage.



Blade of Shadows: Used on the furthest raid member when he applies Discordant Contagion. Deals ~800 damage.



Essence Transfer: Used every ~30 seconds. Lasts 15 seconds. Causes Galenir to heal for 124,562 every time the effected player takes an action.



Adds Spawned:

Mote of Calm: Spawns after he transforms into a werewolf. When killed, spawns a green bubble (deals no damage) that transforms Galenir back into his human form.

Health: 106,464



Strategy:

When the fight starts, Tank #1 will pickup the boss. Tank #2 will be building threat in the meantime. All DPS and healers will be stacked with one raid member 5 meters behind the raid group. Two Bards will form a triangle with Tank #1. They should be 15 meters from Tank#1 and 20 meters from eachother.



At 6 stacks of Infecting Strike, the tanks will swap. This swap will happen multiple times throughout the fight.



When Galenir uses Essence Transfer on a raid member, that member must stop all actions. Moving does not trigger the healing, but attacks, heals, and abilities do. Too many ticks of this will cause your group to not make the enrage timer.



When Galenir uses Discordant Contagion, he will bounce back and forth between the two bards. Healers will dispel anyone who gets Discordant Infection. Raid damage will be high at this point in time.



Galenir will transform into a werewolf every 40-50 seconds, generating a 25% increased ability cost debuff to the whole raid. He will also use Howl of Death every 10 seconds, dealing large raid damage.



After ~3 seconds, a Mote of Calm will spawn. All DPS must focus this down and kill it as quickly as possible. It should be tanked on top of Galenir so that it's aura returns Galenir to human form.



Suggested Raid Comp:

2 Tanks

3 Support (2 Bards, 1 Archon)

3 Cleric Healers

2 Cleric Hybrid Inquis/Justicars

10 DPS
