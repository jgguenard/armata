DOTA 2 AG Open #2 Summary

Article
The AG Open tournament was created in order to help promote the DotA 2 semi-professional scene in the Americas. It's also an opportunity for the amateur teams to improve their skills and make new friends as well.

The second edition was held on April 20th and we'd like to thank all the teams and casters for making it happen ! With 32 teams competing for 325$ in prizes and 3 well-commented live streams, it was definitely a success. By the way, we'd like to thank our friends at eSports Canada for their usual high quality casting during the whole day. We'd like to thank also Maut from NEO, for his professional casting of the finale opposing Typical Mistakes to Meenix eSports.

If you missed the finale, you can watch the replay (with pro comments) : http://www.twitch.tv/neodota/b/393647954.
