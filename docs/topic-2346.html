statick wrote: shadow demon ou shadow shaman avec kunka pour runner en trilane avec disruptor

Pourquoi?

donne moi une raison vraiment solide

Set-up stun (Shackle) > Stun/Slow/Nuke (Torrent) + Nuke (SS's nuke) > Kinetic and/or Nuke (Disruptor).

Lane can push (Wards), Counter-push (SS' nuke, Tidebringer), Harass (Tidebringer, nukes), Kill (^), provides decent teamfight (AoE nukes, 2 instant disables - SS, then good damage and 2 positionned disables - Kunk, and Disruptor 0 if played well - just fucks up teamfights.

Lane is a bit squish and while Kunkka does well with farm, he doesn't really offer much in terms of scaling. He's very bursty. Once the burst is gone his damage isn't sustained, like other carries (AGI, mostly). He also isn't a hero who wants to be in the middle of everything, despite the HP pool. He's more of a periphery hero in a teamfight, blasting his spells once or twice, dealing big damage early. Main weaknesses are perhaps having to wait until level 6 for the true pushing power to begin, and in a defensive tri the Kunkka (Tidebringer) will push the lane a lot harder then one would want.

So as a lane, works fine. As a viable lineup, I'd say not so much. Then again, SD + Kunkka has been used as a very strong dual lane.

TBH, any hero with an instant stun (a lot) + any hero with a deyaled stun (Lina, Leshrac are the two more commons) + nukes = dead people and strong lane.
