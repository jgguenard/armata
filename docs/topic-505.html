[size=medium]Location[/size]

TomorrowWorld takes place at Chattahoochee Hills in South Fulton County. This 8,000-acre paradise is filled with well-established rolling pastures, lakes, and woodlands that run 12 miles along the Chattahoochee River. The property is only 15 miles west of Atlanta´s Hartsfield Airport.

[size=medium]Minimum Age[/size]

The minimum age for entrance into the festival is 21 years of age. We operate a strict entrance policy and you must show a valid government drivers license, ID card, or passport. No exceptions will be made, not even if you are accompanied by an adult. No ID = No Entrance. If you turn 21 during the weekend of TomorrowWorld, you are only allowed in on the day you turned 21.

The following are acceptable forms of Identification for Entry:
-Government Issued Passports
-Driver´s License (USA or Canadian)
-Military Identification Card
-Government Issued Identification Card (USA or Canadian)

The Following Are Unacceptable Forms of Identification for Entry:
-Expired Identification Cards
-School Identification Cards
-Licenses/ID cards issued in countries other than the U.S. or Canada
-Birth Certificates
-Social Security cards
-Federal Immigration cards
-Voter Registration
-Visa Cards
-Copies of any acceptable forms of identification
