Patch 5.1 PTR Notes Update - October 18

Message #1 le 2012-10-18 @ 13:52:23 par Devastaz:
Patch 5.1

Si on résume...

Druid
The cast time of Symbiosis has been reduced to 2 seconds. Petit buff ici, mais rien de majeur.
The Glyph of Solar Beam has been changed, and now increases the radius of Solar Beam to 10 yards (was 5 yards). Si le glyph est utilisé, bonne chance pour sortir du beam.. 10 yards c'est GROS !

Hunter NERFED !
Aspect of the Fox has been removed. Maintenant steady shot se cast en bougeant tout le temps... c'est juste encore plus simple de jouer hunter.
Lynx Rush has been changed. Lynx Rush is now a bleed effect that causes damage every 3 seconds over a period of 15 seconds, and stacks up to 9 times. NERF bien apprécié.
Bestial Wrath no longer grants Hunter pets immunity to crowd control effects, and instead now breaks existing crowd control effects when activated. NERF bien apprécié.
Healing performed by Spirit Beasts summoned by the Stampede talent is now correctly reduced by 75%.
Rabid has been changed and now increases a pet's attack speed by 70%. Pet attack power is now unaffected by this ability.

Mage NERFED HARD !
Mage Armor now reduces the duration of harmful Magic effects by 25% (was 35%).
The damage absorbed by Ice Barrier has been reduced by 25%.
Pyroblast now has a 3 second cooldown.
Ring of Frost and Deep Freeze now have a 45 second cooldown (was 30 seconds).
The mana cost of Spellsteal has been increased to 21% of base mana (was 7%).
Combustion's periodic damage is now based only on the current Ignite, instead of both Ignite and Pyroblast. Damage has been increased to bring it up to approximately the same level as before.

Rogue Petit buff... mais GROS buff sur blind.
The cooldown of Sprint has been reduced to 45 seconds (was 1 minute).
The cooldown of Blind has been reduced to 1 minute (was 3 minutes).
The energy cost of the Burst of Speed talent has been reduced to 50 energy (was 60 energy).
Wound Poison now deals 33% more damage.

Warlock Destro est encore meilleur... BUFF
Conflagrate now generates Burning Embers. Ce qui veut dire, plus de burning embers = + de chaos bolt = + de 1 shot de 300k dmg.
