Titan Alliance is ceasing operations.

There are many reasons for this. These reasons range from guilds retiring from the game, guilds moving on to a new server and new challenges, ANet not addressing server transfers and queues and Titan members wanting to make the game more competitive for the community.

The goal of Titan was to be the most dominant alliance in Guildwars 2 and that goal was met. The guilds of Titan:

ARMA
DIE
CND
DROW
DTD
ELITE

RMA

TRF
ULTD
YAKI

worked together and earned a 30-0 record while fighting at the top tier virtually the entire time. Titan was a perfect 7-0 vs the servers of Ascension Alliance and 7-0 vs Stormbluff Isle.

Those guilds from Titan that have not retired nor transferred are in the process of deciding their next move in order to seek new challenges.

All the members of TA want to thank the community for many epic battles.
Guildmaster of [PRX] The Professionals....since 1999[hr]
Evening

By now we have heard about CND going unofficial and Luciform being no more. There are other Titan guilds that are probably considering leaving the game or have very low activity levels. It's a game after all and people don't play games they don't enjoy: believe me [PRX] has been there. Certainly I have nothing but good to say to both those guilds as they were a big part of our collective success.

Moving forward, I would hope that we don't all go our separate ways, however Titan is not the same Titan as it was at launch. I see three choices right now:

1. Continue with Titan and work to recruit other guilds.
2. Retire the Titan name as GW2's greatest alliance, 30-0 this Friday, and reform as something a little more unofficial but still coordinate amongst leaders and in teamspeaks. I think I like this one the best.
3. Totally break apart and go our separate ways (which I prefer not to do).
