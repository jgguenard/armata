There is really a lot of moving parts to this mob but once you get in sync you'll be surprised how quick he dies. This might not work for everyone but what we did was -

1) Survive as someone posted. Go respec, use endurance gear, use pots, mathos vendor food, and anything else you can to boost your survivability. Our mages and clerics were above 6k hp's as well.

2) The last 30% you should pre-assign spots for people to dps / heal / whatever from. Just have people be ready to collapse for storm. By spreading out you are negating the splash dmg from explosive crystal. Whoever posted it hits the farthest person is spreading bad info. Spread out for explosive + Collapse = win.

3) I tanked the skels at the base of the stairs near the podium. Tanks will notice after the initial pop the have a pattern which is north, south, north, south etc. Face west and pay attention as they spawn they rush the raid and need to be picked up instantly.

Hope that helps those having issues
